Summary

Andy Dufresne, a banker, is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murders of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence. He befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and becomes instrumental in a money-laundering operation led by the corrupt prison warden.

Actors

The Shawshank Redemption stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding.
